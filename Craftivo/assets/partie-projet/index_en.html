About

Our journey to excellence.

  • 2009-2011

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Hair'Aude started as a dream to create a welcoming space where clients could feel pampered and beautiful. From day one, our focus has been on quality service and genuine care.

  • March 2011

    A Salon is Born

    We opened our doors to the community, offering professional hair styling services. Our commitment to excellence quickly built a loyal clientele.

  • December 2015

    Expanding Services

    We expanded to offer comprehensive beauty services, adding aesthetic treatments and nail care to complement our hair styling expertise.

  • July 2020

    Growing Team

    Our team grew to include specialized professionals in hair styling, aesthetics, and nail artistry, ensuring expert care in every service.
